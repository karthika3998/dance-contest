General Eligibility

Age Requirement: Participants must be between 8 to 15 years old as of the Grand Finale date. Proof of age (passport, Emirates ID, etc.) may be requested.
Residency Requirement: Open exclusively to participants residing in the UAE (NRI community).
Previous Winners: Past winners of this contest may not be eligible to re-enter in subsequent editions.
Group Entries: If duets or groups are permitted in future rounds, all members must meet the eligibility criteria. Final decision on group participation rests with the organizing committee.

Registration and Entry

Deadline: All registrations must be submitted via the official Google Form by 31/05/2025. No late entries will be entertained.
Song Submission: Entries must include a link to the song performance (Google Drive, YouTube, etc.). If uploading a file, it must be in a standard format (e.g., MP3, WAV).
Song Duration: Maximum song length should not exceed 5 minutes.
Song Title: The song title must match the title submitted in the registration form. Discrepancies may lead to disqualification.
Languages Accepted: Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Malayalam, Kannada. The language must be clearly mentioned in the registration form.

Song and Performance Guidelines

Originality: Songs must be original or covers of songs from the public domain. Use of copyrighted music is not permitted unless authorized/licensed.
Content Restrictions: Songs must not contain inappropriate, offensive, political, or explicit content. Violations will lead to immediate disqualification.
Performance Type: No lip-syncing is allowed. Submissions must be of live vocal performance, either solo or group (if applicable).
Technical Quality: Sound quality should be clear. While poor quality may affect scoring, it will not result in automatic disqualification.

Submission of Song

Submission Platform: All song entries must be submitted using the Google Form with functional and accessible links.
File Access: Ensure proper sharing permissions are set on uploaded files (Google Drive, YouTube, etc.).
Confirmation: A confirmation email will be sent upon successful submission.
